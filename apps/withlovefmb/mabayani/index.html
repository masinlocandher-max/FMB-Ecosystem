Cultural and historical project

MabayaniHistory. Expression. Remembrance.

Mabayani is dedicated to the stories, language, identity, and memory of Masinloc and the Sambal people. The work is being developed through careful research, source review, and respect for living communities.

Research standard

No invented history.

Claims are not treated as facts because they are repeated. Dates, translations, oral histories, and archival records must be labeled by source status and reviewed before public release.

Interactive learning material will return only when its historical claims and citations are ready for publication.

01

Document

Record language, memory, stories, and materials without stripping away their context.

02

Verify

Separate sourced history, oral tradition, interpretation, and unresolved research questions.

03

Respect

Represent living communities with dignity, consultation, and clear responsibility.
