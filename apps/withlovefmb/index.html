Meet Yoni. A complete space to listen, read, write, and check in.

Yoni is a focused digital wellbeing app. It is not a copy of this website and it does not send users away to read or play content.

Now complete

Yoni by FMB

Your gentle digital companion now has its own full app experience.

Sign in to hear Yoni’s welcome sound, use private check-ins and journaling, play the complete 31-track music catalog, and read all six eBooks in Yoni’s native reader.

  • Private check-ins and dated journal
