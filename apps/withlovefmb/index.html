Meet Yoni. A complete place to listen, read, write and check in.

Yoni keeps its experience focused. Members can use the journal, check in privately, listen to the complete music collection and read all available eBooks inside the app.

Yoni by FMB

A private companion built around calm, reflection and practical support.

Yoni brings reading, original music, check-ins, journaling and grounding into one warm mobile experience. It supports the user without pretending to replace professional or emergency care.

  • Private check-ins and dated journal
  • Complete music library and persistent player
  • Six eBooks with saved reading progress
  • Grounding, Kind Wall, profile and support directory
