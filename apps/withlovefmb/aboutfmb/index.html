01

Building ideas that deserve to be understood.

Francine Marie Bautista builds brands, ideas, platforms, and cultural initiatives designed to become clearer, more meaningful, and harder to ignore.

Her work sits at the intersection of branding, strategic communications, public perception, creativity, entrepreneurship, and community impact. She helps people, businesses, and ideas communicate who they are, why they matter, and how they should be remembered.

Rather than treating creative disciplines as separate, she brings them together to build stronger stories, coherent identities, and platforms that can grow beyond a single campaign or moment.

Rooted in Masinloc.
Building from Metro Manila.

Francine’s identity and creative perspective are deeply shaped by Masinloc, Zambales, Philippines, where her connection to Sambal culture, heritage, community, and local history began.

Her professional work also extends from BGC, Taguig City, Metro Manila, where she continues to build brands, companies, digital platforms, cultural projects, and creative initiatives for a wider audience.

Grounded in where she comes from, while building for what comes next.
