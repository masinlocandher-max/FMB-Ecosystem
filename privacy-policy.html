Privacy and care

A gentle, clear promise.

The With love, FMB community is meant to feel safe, simple, and human. This page explains what happens when you join and share a thought.

What you may share

You may choose a name or nickname, add an email if you wish, and write thoughts in your community space. Your email is optional.

Where your information stays

At this stage, your profile details and submitted thoughts are stored only in the browser on the device you use. The website does not send them to a server or to Francine Marie Bautista.

Because they stay on your device, changing devices, using another browser, or clearing browser data may remove them.

Your thoughts belong to you

Your community space is for expression, not performance. Thoughts are not published publicly, reviewed, or answered. They remain part of your local history until you remove the profile or clear the browser data.

Your choices

You can edit your display name and optional email inside your profile. You can also remove your local profile and its thought history from the device at any time.

Support and urgent help

The community space is not an emergency or counseling service. The Help area keeps crisis and mental health contacts visible so support is easier to reach when it is needed.

Joining should feel like entering a caring room, not signing a complicated contract. This guide exists so you can choose with clarity and comfort.
