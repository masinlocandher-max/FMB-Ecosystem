Privacy Policy

Clear, simple, and respectful.

This page explains what information may be used when profiles and member tools are active.

Creating a profile

A profile may ask for your name or nickname, email address, and password. Your email must be verified before the member page opens.

Private notes

Private notes are meant for the signed-in member who created them. They should not be shown to other members or volunteers.

Community posts

Posts sent to the community are reviewed before they appear. A post may be approved, returned for changes, or not published. Only the name or nickname you choose, the post, and its public date should appear.

Private messages

Messages sent by email go to withlovefmb@gmail.com. They are not placed on a public wall.

Why some records are needed

Basic account and security records may be needed to verify access, stop abuse, protect member data, and respond to account requests. These records should be limited to what the service needs.

Your choices

  • Update your display name.
  • Ask for a copy of your account information.
  • Ask for corrections or deletion.
  • Stop using the member service and request account closure.

Music and Google Drive

The music page uses a connected Google Drive library for approved audio files. Public music should only use files that are intentionally shared for listening.

Emergency help

Emergency numbers and crisis contacts stay public. You do not need to create a profile or pay to see them.

Questions

Email withlovefmb@gmail.com for privacy questions or account requests.
