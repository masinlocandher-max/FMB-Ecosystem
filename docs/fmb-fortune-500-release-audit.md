# FMB Network Fortune 500 Release Audit

This release gate covers the complete public and member-facing FMB ecosystem.

## Route coverage

- Home
- About FMB
- With Love, FMB
- Community Engagements and volunteer workflows
- Get Help and public-support directories
- FMB News landing page and every article route
- FMB Music landing page, library, player and release pages
- FMB eBook landing page, library and reading routes
- FMB&CO.
- SENZ gateway and linked public routes
- Cognita gateway and linked public routes
- Pearly Reception Desk, FAQ, article search and contact routing
- Sign-in, sign-up, member profile and member-only media
- Legal, privacy, installation and PWA routes
- Yoni public landing, installation and authenticated application shell

## Fortune 500 quality gate

Every route must pass:

1. Correct page-specific logo and identity.
2. Approved HD assets only, with no stretching, checkerboard backgrounds or destructive crops.
3. Volunteer and documentary images remain untouched unless explicitly approved.
4. Consistent executive typography, spacing, navigation, footer and responsive grid.
5. iPhone safe areas, tablet layout and desktop scaling.
6. Motion that is restrained, hardware-conscious and disabled through Reduced Motion.
7. Working modals, drawers, player, search, forms and navigation.
8. No broken links, images, console errors, horizontal overflow or duplicate loaders.
9. SEO title, description, canonical, social image, structured data and heading hierarchy.
10. Performance audit for critical CSS, JavaScript, images, fonts, caching and lazy loading.
11. Accessibility audit for keyboard use, focus, labels, contrast and semantic landmarks.
12. Security and privacy review for public forms, member routes and cross-project boundaries.

Production remains untouched until all automated checks and desktop, tablet and iPhone visual reviews pass.
