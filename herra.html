Herra

A companion that must be cared for, too.

Herra is planned as an added digital companion for reflection, simple check-ins, and guided resources. It will not replace a doctor, therapist, emergency service, or trusted person.

Why it will have a fee

Safe service has real costs.

Herra needs secure hosting, privacy protection, careful testing, regular updates, safety checks, and people who keep the system working.

A small fee will help pay for those needs. It also helps keep the free parts of the website open and cared for.

What stays free

Help should still be easy to reach.

Emergency numbers, public help pages, basic reading materials, project information, and the main website will stay open without Herra.

A profile may also include free member tools when the secure account service is active.

Where the support goes

Clear costs, then community value.

Website care

Hosting and security

Keeping the website online, protected, backed up, and working across devices.

People and tools

Maintenance and review

Paying for the services and people needed to test, update, and improve Herra responsibly.

When there is extra

Give it back

When support is more than the service needs, the plan is to use the excess for free materials, community activities, and future service projects.

Built carefully

Herra will open only when the basics are ready.

That includes verified accounts, payment and cancellation rules, privacy controls, safety checks, clear limits, and a way to respond when something goes wrong.

For urgent needs

Real help comes first.

When someone may be in danger, Herra should direct them to emergency and crisis services before anything else.

Open help contacts

Built with care for people, privacy, and community value.
