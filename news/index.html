The FMB editorial standard

Calm is not the absence of urgency. It is the discipline to get the story right.

01

We show the difference.

Reported fact, public allegation, official response, and FMB reflection are never presented as the same thing.

02

We link the record.

Major claims lead back to public documents, official releases, or established reporting so readers can inspect the source.

03

We correct clearly.

Readers can contact the editorial desk. Material corrections should be visible, direct, and made without defensiveness.

Letter from the editor

A newsroom built for understanding.

Francine Marie Bautista created FMB Newsroom to make consequential information easier to understand without turning it into spectacle. News summaries are based on linked public records and established reporting. FMB reflection is opinion and is always labeled.
