The woman behind the work

Francine Marie Bautista

Francine Marie Bautista is a creative director, strategist, storyteller, educator, and founder who builds spaces where people, communities, and meaningful ideas can be seen with dignity.

Her work connects strategic communication, future-ready learning, cultural memory, and human care. Through With love, FMB, she brings these worlds together in one home for advocacy, expression, and possibility.

SENZ Strategic Communications

SENZ exists to help meaningful work become easier to understand and harder to overlook. It brings together strategy, messaging, creative direction, public relations, and digital communication with clarity at the center.

Its deeper purpose is not simply visibility. It is visibility that helps people make informed choices, trust what they are seeing, and recognize the value of an idea.

Cognita Institute of AI

Cognita is an education initiative for people who want to understand artificial intelligence without feeling overwhelmed or left behind.

It approaches AI as a practical human skill, with room for responsibility, creativity, confidence, and thoughtful use.

MABAYANI

MABAYANI is a cultural preservation initiative centered on local history, language, memory, and community participation.

It gives cultural knowledge a digital home so more people can learn, contribute, remember, and carry it forward.
