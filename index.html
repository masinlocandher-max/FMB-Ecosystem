About the founder

Francine Marie Bautista

Francine Marie Bautista is a Filipina transgender brand strategist, creative director, and advocate from Masinloc, Zambales. A proud Zambaleña and Masinloqueña, she also works as an educator, photographer, storyteller, and communications practitioner.

Her work brings together strategy, identity, communication, learning, technology, photography, storytelling, advocacy, and community care. She builds projects that help people and ideas become clearer, stronger, and easier to trust.
