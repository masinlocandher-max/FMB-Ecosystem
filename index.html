Herra

Why Herra will not be free.

Herra needs safe hosting, privacy protection, regular testing, careful updates, and people who keep the service working. A small fee will help cover those real costs.

Emergency contacts, public help pages, and basic reading materials will stay open. Herra will be an added service, not a replacement for professional care.

Where support goes

Maintenance first, community next.

Support will first pay for website care, security, hosting, member tools, and the people who maintain them.

When there is more than what the service needs, the plan is to return the excess through community activities, free materials, and future service projects.
