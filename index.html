What these numbers are for.

Immediate emergency

Call 911

For immediate danger, a medical emergency, violence, fire, police, or ambulance help.

Call 911

Mental health crisis line

NCMH 1553

For overwhelming distress, a mental health crisis, or thoughts of hurting yourself.

Call 1553

NCMH mobile lines

Crisis support

Use these when 1553 does not connect or when calling from a mobile phone.

For a friend

Stay with them

Listen without judging. Keep them near another person. Call 911 when there is immediate danger.

Move closer to help

Go to a trusted person, a clinic, or the nearest emergency room. You do not have to explain everything at once.
