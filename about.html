A life shaped by ideas and people

She turns complex stories into work people can understand and remember.

Francine’s work sits where communication, identity, culture, learning, and public life meet. She is known for seeing the larger story behind a brand, a person, a place, or a community, then finding a clearer way to express it.

Francine Marie Bautista began her professional journey in education and training. Her experience as an instructor and corporate trainer shaped the way she communicates today: structured enough to guide people, warm enough to make learning feel possible, and practical enough to turn ideas into action.

Her background in tourism management gave her an early understanding of place, culture, experience, and public perception. She later expanded her work into branding, public relations, creative direction, photography, content, digital products, and strategic communications.

As a founder, she does not build projects only to look impressive. She builds systems that respond to a need. SENZ helps people and organizations communicate with greater clarity. Cognita Institute of AI helps learners understand and use artificial intelligence with confidence. MABAYANI protects local history, language, and memory by giving cultural knowledge a digital home.

Francine also writes and speaks about identity, visibility, becoming, human behavior, and the quiet work behind building a meaningful life. Her voice is direct but caring. She believes people deserve language that helps them understand themselves, not language that makes them feel small.

Her work is not about making noise. It is about helping the right truth become visible.

Based in Zambales

Rooted in place, built for a wider world.

Francine is based in Masinloc, Zambales. Her work carries the perspective of someone who understands both local community life and the demands of modern digital communication.

She brings the discipline of a strategist, the eye of a creative director, the patience of an educator, and the care of a cultural advocate into every project.

Her goal is not to separate business from humanity. It is to build work where clarity, dignity, and purpose can exist together.

Professional path

A career built across education, communication, and culture.

Education and training

Francine studied Tourism Management and completed professional teaching units. She worked as an instructor and later as a trainer in corporate and local government settings, helping learners build practical communication and service skills.

Creative work

Her work expanded into photography, visual storytelling, graphic design, hosting, scriptwriting, music, content development, social media, and creative production.

Strategy and PR

She developed a deeper practice in branding, public relations, narrative, reputation, campaign direction, and digital presence. This became the foundation of SENZ Strategic Communications.

Technology and learning

Through Cognita Institute of AI, she brings together her experience as an educator and strategist to help people learn artificial intelligence without fear or unnecessary complexity.

History and heritage

Through MABAYANI, she documents and studies the history, language, identity, and cultural memory of Masinloc and its communities, while protecting accuracy and clearly identifying research gaps.
