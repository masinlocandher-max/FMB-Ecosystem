The woman behind the work

Francine Marie Bautista

A Filipina founder, speaker, author, brand strategist, creative director, educator, and cultural historian building work that helps people, ideas, and communities become clearer, stronger, and harder to overlook.

Her work

She helps people see what matters.

Francine works where communication, identity, culture, learning, technology, and public life meet.

Francine Marie Bautista turns complex ideas into clear stories, useful systems, and work people can understand. She looks beyond appearance. She studies what an audience needs to know, what a project must stand for, and how every part should work together.

Her background in teaching and training shaped the way she leads. She values structure, clear language, and practical learning. Her experience in creative work shaped the way she sees emotion, visual identity, public perception, and the small details people remember.

She has worked across education, training, photography, branding, public relations, content, digital products, hosting, writing, and creative direction. These fields are not separate in her practice. They support one goal: helping meaningful work become easier to see, trust, and remember.

Her point of view

Clear work can still feel human.

Francine believes strategy should not remove warmth. Good communication can be careful, direct, beautiful, and easy to understand at the same time.

She builds with purpose, protects factual accuracy, and avoids making people feel small just to make a message sound important.

What she brings

Different roles, one clear way of thinking.

Founder

She builds the whole system.

Francine develops the idea, identity, structure, message, experience, and path needed to move a project forward.

Speaker and Author

She gives language to real experiences.

Her talks and writing focus on identity, visibility, confidence, culture, communication, and becoming.

Brand Strategist

She finds the clearest position.

She studies audience, reputation, public perception, identity, and communication gaps before deciding what a brand should say and show.

Creative Director

She protects the full story.

She guides visuals, photography, messaging, content, and campaigns so every part feels connected.

Educator

She makes hard topics easier to learn.

Her experience as an instructor and trainer informs the way she designs lessons, materials, workshops, and learning platforms.

Cultural Historian

She protects memory with care.

Her historical work focuses on Masinloc, Tina Sambal language, local memory, church history, oral traditions, archival gaps, and the difference between verified records and stories passed down by the community.

Professional path

A career shaped by teaching, communication, and culture.

Education and training

Francine studied Tourism Management and completed professional teaching units. She worked as an instructor and later as a trainer in corporate and local government settings.

Creative and strategic work

Her work grew into photography, visual storytelling, graphic design, hosting, scriptwriting, content, social media, brand strategy, public relations, and digital products.

Founder-led projects

SENZ Strategic Communications helps people and organizations communicate with greater clarity. Cognita Institute of AI helps learners understand and use artificial intelligence with confidence. MABAYANI gives local history, language, and memory a digital home.

Based in Zambales

Francine is based in Masinloc, Zambales. Her work carries the perspective of someone who understands local community life and the demands of a wider digital world.
